Wine, Dine and Unwind At Dine

Located to the west of Edinburgh city centre, in the neighbourhoods of Murrayfield, Ravelston, Roseburn and Corstorphine, you will find Dine, a local community restaurant.

About Us

Open every day, serving breakfast, lunch, brunch and evening meals, alongside a carefully curated wine, drinks and cocktail list. Exclusive events with Charles Heidsieck, Edinburgh Gin, Kingsbarns Distillery and Darnley’s Gin, plus guest chef takeovers and gourmet extravaganzas, what’s not to love!

Our food quality

Menus designed by award-winning Michelin starred chef Stuart Muir and his team of talented chefs, celebrating the very best of nature’s harvest, with a firm focus on provenance and supporting local independent suppliers from Edinburgh, Fife, the Borders and beyond.
